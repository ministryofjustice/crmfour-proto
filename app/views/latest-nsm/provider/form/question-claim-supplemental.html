Make a supplemental claim

You must send supplemental claims by email or post.

What to include

  • Non-standard fee magistrates’ court claim form (CRM7)
  • A copy of the representation order, plus any new or amended orders
  • Evidence of extra work – for example, file notes or disbursement invoices

Include additional information related to how the original claim was made.
